Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you connect your LinkedIn account, we store:

  • Your LinkedIn profile ID
  • Encrypted OAuth access token

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data solely to:

  • Enable automatic posting to your LinkedIn profile
  • Maintain the connection between our dashboard and LinkedIn

3. Data Security

  • All tokens are encrypted using AES encryption
  • We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Your data is never shared with third parties

4. Your Rights

  • Disconnect LinkedIn anytime in profile settings
  • Request data deletion by contacting us
  • Access your stored data on request
